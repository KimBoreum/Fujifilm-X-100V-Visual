The timeless design of the X100V is highlighted by the sharp lines of the top cover. The specially
processed aluminum exterior delivers a metal texture to your fingertips, a sensation that is
difficult to find on a painted body. The goal of the X100V is not merely to function as a camera.
It aims to be the ultimate partner that reignites the joy and depth of photography.

The X100V is equipped with a newly designed
23mmF2 lens that maximizes the capabilities
of the 'X-Trans CMOS 4' sensor.
It delivers high resolution from the open aperture,
boasting excellent definition from the minimum
shooting distance to infinity.
Additionally, by mounting the wide conversion
lens 'WCL-X100II' and the teleconversion lens
'TCL-X100II', the expressive power of the X100V
is further enhanced, rivaling the versatility of
interchangeable lens cameras.

200 FILM

This film is suitable for portrait photography,
and its vivid, over-color palette is useful for
general and outdoor photography. It has a
nominal sensitivity of ISO 200/27°, making it
suitable for both scanning and enlargement
applications.

Look at with a fresh eye

The 'OVF', which satisfies photographers'
fundamental desire to see the subject with their own
eyes, and the 'EVF', which allows for easy
verification of the finished photograph reflecting all
elements such as exposure, white balance, and
color reproduction by film simulation. The system
that enables switching between these with just one
lever can truly be described as the 'ideal state of
viewfinders'. The viewfinder of the X100V has
evolved further. The OVF, which provides a
parallax-corrected frame line, minimizes distortion
and enhances the field of view to present the world
as it is with a clear sight.

Battery Performance

The number of standard shots has significantly increased
compared to previous models. When using the EVF, you can
continuously shoot approximately 350 shots, and with the OVF,
up to about 420 shots.
